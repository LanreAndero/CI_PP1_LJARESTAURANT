We strive to showcase the rich culinary traditions of Nigeria through a menu that features a wide variety of traditional dishes, including jollof rice, fried rice, pounded yam, amala (cassava plantain or yam four), garri (cassava grains), beans, plantain, vegetable soup, okra soup, egusi soup, oha soup, ogbono soup, afang soup, onugbu (bitter leaf) soup, suya, isi ewu (goat head), and much more. Each dish is carefully prepared using fresh ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to deliver an explosion of flavours that will delight your taste buds.
