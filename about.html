About Us

Welcome to LJA Restaurant, your gateway to the vibrant flavors of Nigeria. Our restaurant is located in Dundalk, Co. Louth, Ireland, and offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere to enjoy authentic Nigerian cuisine.


Our chefs meticulously prepare each dish, ensuring that the flavors and traditions of Nigerian cooking shine through. From the famous Jollof Rice to the mouthwatering Suya, our menu offers a wide range of options to satisfy your cravings.


Experience the warmth of Nigerian hospitality and indulge in the rich culinary heritage of Nigeria at the Nigerian Restaurant in Dundalk.
